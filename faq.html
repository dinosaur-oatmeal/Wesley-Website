Frequently Asked Questions

What is the UTA Wesley?

We are an open, affirming, and inclusive campus ministry of the United Methodist Church. We exist to build a beloved community of disciples, impacting the world through authentic expression and radical grace.

Do you have a building on campus?

We do! Find us at 311 UTA Blvd. right across from the University Center. We're open Mon-Fri, 9AM to 5PM, with a full kitchen, ping pong, and spaces to hang out.

What do you believe?

We celebrate diversity in all forms, believing everyone is created in God’s image and deserves respect. For a deeper dive, visit our Beliefs page.

How do I sign up?

No fees, no requirements. Everything we do is open to every student at UTA. Just show up or follow us on social media!

Is Wesley a church?

Yes, but with a different focus. We are "Connectional"—existing to connect students to Christ, college, and community. We also help students engage with local churches!

Can I park in your lot?

Absolutely! We have 11 pay-to-park spots for students on a first-come, first-serve basis. After 5pm and on weekends, the university lot south of us is also available.
